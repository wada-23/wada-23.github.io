About me

Hi, I'm Walid, a passionate furniture designer with over 3 years of experience in creating functional, aesthetically pleasing, and sustainable furniture designs. My work is driven by a deep appreciation for craftsmanship, materials, and the stories they tell. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics but also about creating pieces that enhance the lives of those who use them.

I specialize in modern and minimalist designs, often incorporating natural materials like wood, metal, and glass. My designs are inspired by the balance between form and function, and I strive to create pieces that are both beautiful and practical.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy exploring new materials, experimenting with 3D modeling, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in furniture design. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate with clients to bring their visions to life.
